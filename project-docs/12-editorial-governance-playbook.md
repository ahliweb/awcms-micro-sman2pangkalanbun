# Editorial Governance Playbook (E2 / #12)

This playbook defines publishing workflow, approvals, correction policy, archival policy, and urgent announcement SLAs.

## Workflow and Roles

- `Contributor/Author`:
	- Drafts content
	- Performs initial fact check and metadata completion
- `Editor`:
	- Reviews content quality and policy compliance
	- Approves publish-ready drafts
- `Admin`:
	- Handles escalations, urgent overrides, and final governance decisions

## Publishing Workflow

1. Draft created by author.
2. Editorial review by editor.
3. Revision loop until approved.
4. Publish by editor/admin.
5. Post-publish monitoring for corrections.

## Correction Policy

- Minor corrections (typos/format): update directly, preserve revision history.
- Material corrections (facts/dates/claims):
	- update content,
	- append correction note,
	- notify editor/admin in issue log.

## Archival Policy

- Archive content when outdated but historically relevant.
- Keep URL stable where possible; avoid destructive deletion.
- Use status changes and taxonomy flags to remove from active listings.

## SLA Targets for Urgent Announcements

- Draft to review: within `30 minutes`.
- Review to publish: within `30 minutes`.
- Correction on urgent item: within `15 minutes` of verified report.

## Tabletop Review Scenarios

- Urgent school closure announcement.
- Incorrect published event date correction.
- Archive of stale policy announcement with retained public record.
